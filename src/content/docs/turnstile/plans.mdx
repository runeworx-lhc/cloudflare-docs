Cloudflare Turnstile is available on the Free and Enterprise plans. 

- **Free**: Best for personal websites and blogs, small to medium businesses, development and testing environments, and most production applications.
- **Enterprise**: Best for large enterprises with high-volume traffic, organizations requiring advanced bot detection and device fingerprinting, organizations requiring custom branding, companies with strict compliance requirements, and multi-domain or complex hosting environments.

<FeatureTable id="security.turnstile" />

## Additional features

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Free">
    - **All widget types**: Managed, Non-interactive, and Invisible
    - **Unlimited challenges**: No limit on traffic or verification requests
    - **WCAG 2.1 AA compliance**: Full accessibility support
    - **Community support**: Access to Cloudflare Community forums
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Enterprise">
    - **Ephemeral ID**: Advanced bot detection using Ephemeral IDs for enhanced security
    - **Custom branding**: Remove Cloudflare branding and customize widget appearance
    - **Advanced analytics**: Extended lookback periods and detailed metrics
    - **Priority support**: Dedicated support channels
    - **Advanced hostname management**: Flexible hostname configuration options
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
